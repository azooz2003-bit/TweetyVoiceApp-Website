## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I connect my X account?**
A: Tap "Login with X" on the authentication screen. You'll be redirected to X's official login page to authorize Tweety Voice. Your credentials are never shared with us.

**Q: What permissions does Tweety need?**
A: Tweety requires access to read your timeline, post tweets, reply, like, follow, and send direct messages. These permissions are necessary to provide full voice-control functionality.

**Q: Do I need a subscription?**
A: Yes, Tweety Voice requires an active subscription. Your subscription provides $8 in credits each week that refresh automatically. You can also purchase additional credits if needed.

### Using Voice Commands

**Q: How do I post a tweet with my voice?**
A: Simply say something like "Post a tweet saying..." followed by your message. The AI will confirm before posting.

**Q: What voice commands are supported?**
A: Tweety Voice supports comprehensive X control through voice. Here are all available commands:

**Posts & Tweets**
- Create/post tweets
- Reply to tweets
- Quote tweets
- Create poll tweets
- Delete tweets
- Edit tweets
- View specific tweets or multiple tweets
- Get your tweets or someone else's tweets
- Get your mentions
- View your home timeline
- Search recent tweets (last 7 days)
- Search all tweets (full archive)

**Users & Following**
- Get user details by ID or username
- View your own profile
- See who someone follows
- See someone's followers
- Follow/unfollow users
- Mute/unmute users
- View muted users list
- Block/unblock DMs from users
- View blocked users list

**Likes & Engagement**
- Like/unlike tweets
- See who liked a tweet
- View tweets you've liked
- View tweets someone else liked

**Retweets**
- Retweet/unretweet posts
- See who retweeted a tweet
- View retweets of a specific tweet
- See reposts of your tweets

**Lists**
- Create, delete, or update lists
- View list details and tweets from lists
- Add/remove members from lists
- Follow/unfollow lists
- Pin/unpin lists
- View pinned, owned, or followed lists
- View list followers and memberships

**Direct Messages**
- Send DMs to users
- Create group DM conversations
- View all recent DMs
- View specific conversations
- View your entire conversation history with someone
- Delete DM messages

**Bookmarks**
- Add/remove bookmarks
- View your bookmarked tweets

**Trends**
- Get personalized trending topics

**News**
- Get news by ID
- Search news stories

**Q: The AI misunderstood my command. What should I do?**
A: Use clear and specific voice commands. Review important actions before confirming them. You can always manually correct or delete any posts made in error on X.

### Account & Billing

**Q: How do credits work?**
A: Your subscription provides $8 in credits each week that refresh automatically. Credits are consumed based on AI voice processing usage. If you exhaust your weekly credits, you can purchase additional credits for extended usage.

**Q: What happens to unused credits?**
A: Credits refresh weekly with your subscription. Unused credits from the previous week do not roll over - you get a fresh $8 allocation each week.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to Settings > Subscriptions in the iOS Settings app, select Tweety Voice, and choose "Cancel Subscription." Your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing period.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund requests are handled by Apple according to their refund policy. Visit the App Store or contact Apple Support for assistance.

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Is my voice data stored?**
A: No. Voice recordings are processed in real-time by our AI partners (Grok and OpenAI) and are not permanently stored by Tweety Voice.

**Q: How is my X account secured?**
A: We use OAuth 2.0 authentication with tokens stored securely in your device's Keychain with hardware-backed encryption. We never see your X password.

**Q: Can I revoke Tweety's access to my X account?**
A: Yes. You can revoke access anytime through X's settings under "Apps and sessions" or by logging out of Tweety Voice.

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem:** "Login failed" or "Something went wrong" when connecting X account
**Solution:**
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Try logging out and back in to your X account in Safari
- Check that your X account is not suspended or restricted
- Restart the app and try again

**Problem:** "Unauthorized" error after logging in
**Solution:**
- Your session may have expired. Log out and log back in
- Check if your X account has any security restrictions
- Ensure Tweety Voice is authorized in your X account settings

### Voice Recognition Issues

**Problem:** Voice commands not working or not recognized
**Solution:**
- Check microphone permissions: Settings > Tweety Voice > Microphone
- Ensure you're in a quiet environment
- Speak clearly and at a normal pace
- Check your internet connection (voice processing requires internet)

**Problem:** AI performs wrong action
**Solution:**
- Use more specific commands
- Review the action before confirming when prompted
- Check your X timeline to verify the action
- Manually correct any errors directly on X

### App Performance

**Problem:** App crashes or freezes
**Solution:**
- Restart the app
- Ensure you're running the latest version from the App Store
- Restart your device
- If the issue persists, contact support with details

**Problem:** Slow response from voice assistant
**Solution:**
- Check your internet connection speed
- Close other apps using network resources
- Try again during off-peak hours
