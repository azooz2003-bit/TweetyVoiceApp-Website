### `Latest Release`
# **Version 1.0** - Now Available on the App Store!

Tweety Voice is now live on the App Store! Control X (Twitter) with your voice using advanced AI-powered voice assistants.

#### Features
- Real-time voice chat with AI assistants (Grok and OpenAI)
- Post tweets, reply, like, and manage your X account hands-free
- Secure X OAuth2 authentication with App Attest
- Low-latency audio streaming for natural conversations
- Usage analytics to track API consumption
- Flexible subscription model with pay-as-you-go credits
